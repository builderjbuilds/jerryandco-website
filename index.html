How it works

The Jerry & Co. factory-finish process

Every cabinet refinishing project follows the same five stages — no surprises, no guesswork.

01

Video walkthrough

A 20-minute video call gets us a line-item estimate the same day — no waiting on a site visit.

02

Prep & containment

Cabinets are removed, labeled, and your kitchen is fully masked and dust-contained.

03

Hand sand & repair

Every surface is sanded and any dents, gaps, or chips are filled and leveled.

04

2K spray finish

A factory-grade polyurethane finish is sprayed in a controlled environment for a smooth, durable result.

05

Cure & walkthrough

Doors cure, hardware is reinstalled, and we walk the finished space with you before we call it done.
